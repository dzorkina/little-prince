About Me

Welcome to my creative journey! My name is Daria, and I’m a graphic designer with a passion for storytelling through visuals. The Little Prince has always been a source of inspiration for me—a timeless tale filled with wonder, wisdom, and imagination. When I set out to design this website, I wanted to capture the magic of the book while creating an immersive and visually engaging experience.

Design Concept

From the soft, dreamy colors to the delicate typography, every detail of this website was chosen to reflect the whimsical world of The Little Prince. I aimed to create a space where visitors can not only explore the story but also feel its emotions—curiosity, nostalgia, and the beauty of seeing the world through a child’s eyes.
